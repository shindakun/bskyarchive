About

Bluesky Personal Archive Tool

A local-first archival solution for Bluesky. This tool allows you to maintain a complete, searchable archive of your Bluesky content on your own machine.

Core Principles

  • Data Privacy & Local-First: All data stays on your machine
  • Comprehensive & Accurate: Complete archive of posts, media, and metadata
  • Multiple Export Formats: JSON, CSV, and more
  • Fast & Efficient: Full-text search with SQLite FTS5
  • Incremental Operations: Only fetch new content on updates

Technology

  • Go 1.21+ for performance and reliability
  • SQLite with FTS5 for efficient storage and search
  • AT Protocol via the Indigo SDK
  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
